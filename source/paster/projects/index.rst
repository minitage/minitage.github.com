Getting cgwb up & running
----------------------------------
We use cgwb to generate projects, just launch it and enjoy::
Goal is to choose a template, answer to questions and get a generated tarball from there.

    ./minitagatool.sh cgwb

Then naviguate to `CGWB <http://localhost:8085>`_

Stop it by hitting **<C-C>**
